# Roadmap Planning Workflow

## Goal
Create a strategic product roadmap that aligns with business objectives and user needs.

## Quick Steps

1. **Define Vision & Strategy**
   - Product vision statement
   - Strategic objectives (OKRs)

2. **Gather Inputs**
   - Market analysis, customer feedback
   - Technical constraints, business goals

3. **Prioritize Initiatives**
   - Apply RICE scoring framework
   - Consider dependencies and resources

4. **Structure Timeline**
   - Now (0-3 months), Next (3-6 months), Later (6+ months)
   - Define milestones and key deliverables

5. **Communicate Roadmap**
   - Create visual representations
   - Stakeholder presentations

6. **Review & Iterate**
   - Quarterly reviews and adjustments

## Key Frameworks

- **Now-Next-Later:** Time-based prioritization
- **OKRs:** Objectives and Key Results for alignment
- **RICE:** Reach, Impact, Confidence, Effort scoring

## Success Factors

- Clear connection to business objectives
- Flexible enough to adapt to changes
- Realistic resource allocation
- Stakeholder buy-in achieved
- Measurable success metrics defined
